# Stark Character Traits V1.1

## Faction Trait: House Stark Faction Trait
Wolves' Last Howl - This Character must make all Injury, Death, or Capture Saves before any enemy Characters do so at the conclusion of battle.  If this Character fails any Save, choose one enemy Character, they suffer -2 to their Save of the same type for this battle.  Each enemy Character may only be targeted by one instance of Wolves' Last Howl per battle.

## Arya Stark
- **Faction:** Stark/ Neutral
- **Type:** NCU
- **Trained in Braavos** — When the Starks bid on Arya as a Neutral character they do so with a +2 Coin equivalent credit.  If the Starks win the bid, Arya may be played as her Stark or Neutral versions.  If Arya's Attachment Ability caused another Attachment to be removed from play during this battle, that Character suffers -1 to Death Save for this battle.
- **Wayward Daughter** — The first time Arya is Injured or Killed serving the Starks she is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. She does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately switches to the Neutral.  This may also occur by the Stark Strategy.

## Bran & Hodor
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Summer's Protection** — If Summer was still in play at the conclusion of the battle, Bran & Hodor are immune to any negative modifiers for Injury, Death, or Capture rolls at the conclusion of that battle.

## Brynden Tully
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Intrepid Guide** — When this character is Commanding they may pass through 1 region of impassible terrain during Challenge Phase.
- **Scout Hunter** — Bryden may always re-roll Capture Saves.  Whenever Brynden is deployed in an army, you may cancel the enemy's effect of (16) Keen Scouting.

## Catelyn Stark
- **Faction:** Stark / Brotherhood
- **Type:** NCU
- **Heart of Stone** — The first time Catelyn is Injured or Killed serving the Starks she is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. She does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately switches to the Brotherhood Without Banners.  This may also occur by the Stark Strategy.
- **Northern Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the North, gain +1 Might.

## Eddard's Honor Guard
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Unit
- **Handpicked Men** — Record how many Wounds this unit has sustained at the end of the battle.  If this unit participates in a battle next Month, unless that battle is in the North, it begins that battle with the name number of suffered Wounds.
- **Northman Spirit** — If this unit takes part in a battle outside the North gain +2 Might.  If this unit takes part in a battle in the North gain +3 Might.

## Eddard Stark
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Northern Warden** — When Eddard participates in a battle, gain +4 Might for defensive Challenge victories in the North or offensive Challenge victories outside the North.
- **Highest Honor** — Eddard may not be used in (20) Lavish Tourney but gains an additional +1 to rolls for Trials by Combat.

## Greatjon Umber
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.
- **Bloody Tough** — The first time Greatjon fails a Death Save he may re-roll it once per Campaign.  If he succeeds on that re-roll, gain +3 Might.

## Howland Reed
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Legendary Guide** — Each Month Howland may grant any Stark Commander (including himself) the ability to pass through one Region of impassible terrain during Challenge Phase.  When you declare this effect, note that he must be deployed in the battle if available.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still in play at the end of the battle, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.

## Jaqen H'ghar
- **Faction:** Stark/ Neutral
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Three Names** — Jaqen may use the following ability three times in the service of your faction.  Each faction who gains his service must keep track of their own uses of this ability when they control him.  When Jaqen participates in a battle and is still in play at the end of the battle he may target one enemy Character who is making a Death save.  That Character suffers a -2 to their roll.
- **Un-Name Me** — If Arya Stark is in the Stark faction, the Starks may bid on Jaqen with a +2 Coin equivalent credit.  The Starks may deploy Jaqen as any version.  If Jaqen H'ghar, Mysterious Prisoner is deployed and is out of play at the conclusion of a battle, roll an Injury Save.  If he passes he does not have to roll the usual Death and Capture Saves.

## Jojen Reed
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Easily Overlooked** — This Character is immune to any negative effects against their Capture Saves.

## Lyanna Mormont
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** NCU
- **Northern Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the North, gain +1 Might.
- **Bear Island Ferries** — When Lyanna participates in a Sea Battles, declare one of your Stark Combat units before deployment.  That unit takes -1 Wound from the Torrent keyword.

## Maege Mormont
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Bear Island Values** — When Maege participates in Sea Battles or battles in island Regions you may declare one other friendly Character at the start of the battle.  If Maege is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, that Character may re-roll one Injury, Capture, or Death Save at the conclusion of that battle.  When Maege is Commanding, Sack may not be chosen and Conquering costs 1 fewer Might.
- **Only a Woman** — After the Stark player rolls an Event, if Maege is available you may replace that Event with (5) Lure the Garrison, then place Maege on the board for the Challenge.

## Meera Reed
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Rudimentary Healing** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a battle and is still in play, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  The character gains +1 to one of those tests.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Osha
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Rudimentary Healing** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  The character gains +1 to one of those tests.
- **Interceptor** — When this character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.

## Rickard Karstark
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Thirst for War** — After the Stark player rolls an Event, if Rickard is available you may replace that Event with (14) Land Grab, then place Rickard on the board for the Challenge.  When Rickard is Commanding as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one The Long War card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Rickon Stark
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Black Wolf's Protection** — If Rickon is Injured, Captured, or Killed at the conclusion of the battle when Shaggydog participates, choose one enemy Commander or Attachment who was still in play at the conclusion of the battle, they must make a Death Save.
- **Just a Boy** — If Shaggydog and Rickon participate in a battle and are both in play at the end of the battle, gain +2 Might.  Rickon suffers -2 to Capture saves unless Shaggydog was still in play at the end of the battle.

## Robb Stark
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment/NCU
- **Young Wolf's Cunning** — After the Stark player rolls an Event, if Robb is available you may replace that Event with (9) Clever Ruse, then place Robb on the board for the Challenge.  When Robb is Commanding in a Region controlled by the faction of the Commander opposing him, enemies do not gain any Commander bonuses against him.
- **Intrepid Guide** — When this Character is Commanding they may pass through one Region of impassible terrain during Challenge Phase.

## Rodrik Cassel
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Cautious Marshall** — When Rodrik participates in a battle, Securing costs no Might.  When Rodrik is Commanding as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Rain of Arrows card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck
- **Rigorous Discipline** — When this character participates in a battle, friendly Characters are immune to the effects of (2) Drunken Revelry.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll a Capture Save.

## Sansa Stark
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** NCU
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Northern Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the North, gain +1 Might.

## Syrio Forel
- **Faction:** Stark
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Like Water** — If Syrio passes a Death Save, choose one enemy Attachment who participated in the battle and was not injured, that character must make an Injury Save at a -3 to their roll.
- **Rigorous Discipline** — When this character participates in a battle, friendly units are immune to the effects of (2) Drunken Revelry.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll a Capture Save.
