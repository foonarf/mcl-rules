# Brotherhood Without Banners Character Traits V1.1

## Faction Trait: Brotherhood Without Banners Faction Trait
Phantom Banditry - Each version of this Character may move any distance on the Campaign Map (although Commanders must still declare their path between locations).  If this is not a Neutral Character, each version of this Character may appear in two battles per Month.

## Anguy
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Can't Miss** — When Anguy participates in an offensive siege battle, his army gains the effects of (7) Downed Ravens.
- **Tournament Fighter** — Each time this character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney, they may re-roll one die during that Event.

## Beric Dondarrion
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Zealous Devotion** — When this Beric is Commanding as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Rally the Troops card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.
- **Favor of R'hllor** — Beric may re-roll one die each time he participates in a Trial by Combat.  Beric may re-roll failed Death Saves once per Month as long as Thoros is not Dead or Captured.

## Catelyn Stark
- **Faction:** Brotherhood / Stark
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Vicious Undeath** — When Lady Stoneheart is Commanding and not fighting the Starks, at the conclusion of the battle, Brotherhood Character must roll Saves before their opponents.  Each time a Brotherhood character fails a Save, declare one enemy character who was involved in the battle, they suffer -2 to any Saves they must make as a result of the battle.
- **Heart of Stone** — The first time Catelyn Is injured for the Starks she is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. She does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately switches to the Brotherhood Without Banners.  This may also occur by the Stark Strategy.

## Edric Dayne
- **Faction:** Brotherhood / Martell
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Adventurous Youth** — Edric begins the campaign as a Martell character.  Each time the Martells conclude a battle against the Brotherhood Without Banners, Edric switches to ownership by the other faction.
- **Dornish Lord** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +2 Might.  If the battle took place in Dorne, gain an additional +2 Might.

## Gendry
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.
- **Honorable Heritage** — Each time Gendry participates in a battle, gain +3 Justice.  On a victory, gain an additional +3 Justice.

## Ghost of High Heart
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** NCU
- **Fatal Prophecy** — Once per Month during Challenge Phase when a Challenge has been set and the Brotherhood is not participating in a battle, declare a Commander participating in that battle.  That Commander suffers -2 to Death Saves at the end of that battle.  If the Commander dies at the end of the battle, gain +10 Justice.  If the Commander survives, their faction gains +4 Might.
- **Experienced Healer** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  The character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.

## Hot Pie
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Food for the Fight** — When Hot Pie participates in a battle, at the start of that battle, declare one friendly Character.  The Character gains +1 to all Saves for the resolution of this battle.
- **Easily Overlooked** — This character is immune to any negative effects against their Capture Saves.

## Lem Lemoncloak
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.
- **Interceptor** — When this character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.

## Ravella Smallwood
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** NCU
- **Riverlands Noble** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Riverlands, gain an additional +1 Might.
- **Capable Stewardship** — When this Character participates in a battle as an NCU and still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Coin.

## Thoros of Myr
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Deliverance of R'hllor** — Each time Thoros participates in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, choose one other friendly Character before your opponent declares any negative modifiers against that Character.  They are immune to negative modifiers to all Saves for this battle.
- **Flame in the Breach** — The first time Thoros passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.  When Thoros participates as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Press the Assault card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck.

## Tom Sevenstrings
- **Faction:** Brotherhood
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Irrepressible** — Unless Tom is named as the target of (15) Sequester Foe, all friendly characters are immune to the effects of that Event for any battle he participates in.  When you declare this effect, note that he must be deployed in the battle if available.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.
