# House Stark - Strategy Sheet

## Faction Overview
House Stark of the North may choose to draw strength from a sequence of Sacrifices and Retributions over the course of the Campaign. The Starks may choose to trigger the Sacrifices listed here at the end of Months 1, 2, and 3. Each Sacrifice includes a significant Might bonus and unlocks a set of list size bonuses that remain accessible for the duration of the Campaign. House Stark may utilize list size bonuses from one unlocked Sacrifice and one unlocked Retribution each time an army is created.

## Special Rules and Combat Modifiers

### Homefront Banner
- When fighting battles in the North, gain Stark Commander +1.
- The first time the Starks suffer a defeat in the North, select the enemy faction from that battle. Gain +2 Might from future victories against that faction.

### Sojourning Heroes
- Stark Veteran units ignore location restrictions.
- Once per Campaign Month, one Stark Veteran unit may participate in two different battles in the same Month.

### March of Honor
- When fighting battles outside of the North, gain Stark Commander +1 for each Combat unit included from a different Affiliation.
- Affiliations include: Crannogman, Umber, Mormont, Tully, Karstark.

## Path of the Wolves: Sacrifices
These may be declared at the end of the specified months.

### Sacrifice I (End of Month 1)
- **Condition:** Declare if Eddard Stark has been Killed or you may remove him from the Campaign now.
- **Immediate Penalty:** Lose control of Riverrun, the Whispering Woods, and any unlocked Tully Region Developments in those Regions.
- **Bonus:** Gain +6 Might.
- **List Size Bonuses Unlocked:**
  - House Tully Sworn Shield +1
  - House Tully Cavaliers +1
  - Stark Sworn Swords +1
  - Brynden Tully +2
  - Rodrik Cassel +2

### Sacrifice II (End of Month 2)
- **Condition:** Declare if Arya Stark has been Killed or you may remove her from the Campaign now.
- **Immediate Penalty:** The Starks may bid on Neutral Characters with a +2 Coin equivalent credit.
- **Bonus:** Gain +6 Might.
- **List Size Bonuses Unlocked:**
  - Stark Outriders +1
  - Robb Stark +3
  - Grey Wind +1
  - Shaggydog +1
  - Summer +1

### Sacrifice III (End of Month 3)
- **Condition:** Declare if Robb Stark and Catelyn Stark have been Killed or you may remove Robb Stark and Catelyn Stark from your faction now.
- **Immediate Penalty:** Lose control of Winterfell and the Winter Town even if you control them. Winter Town is Sacked.
- **Bonus:** Gain +6 Might.
- **List Size Bonuses Unlocked:**
  - Crannogman Bog Devils +1
  - Crannogman Survivalist +1
  - Crannogman Trackers +1
  - Crannogman Warden +1
  - Howland Reed +2

## Path of the Wolves: Retributions
These may be declared at the start of the specified months if previous sacrifices were made.

### Retribution I (Start of Month 4)
- **Condition:** Declare if Eddard was Sacrificed in Month 1.
- **Bonus:** Gain +12 Might from battles fought in the North and the Riverlands this Month.
- **List Size Bonuses Unlocked:**
  - Umber Champion +1
  - Umber Berserkers +1
  - Umber Greataxes +1
  - Umber Ravagers +1
  - Greatjon Umber +2

### Retribution II (Start of Month 5)
- **Condition:** Declare if Arya was Sacrificed in Month 2.
- **Bonus:** Gain +12 Might from battles fought in the North and the Riverlands this Month.
- **List Size Bonuses Unlocked:**
  - Karstark Spearmen +1
  - Karstark Loyalists +1
  - Rickard Karstark +2
  - Loyalist Captain +1
  - Stark Bowmen +1

### Retribution III (Start of Month 6)
- **Condition:** Declare if Robb and Catelyn were Sacrificed in Month 3.
- **Bonus:** Gain +12 Might from battles fought in the North and the Riverlands this Month.
- **List Size Bonuses Unlocked:**
  - Winterfell Champion +1
  - Mormont She-Bears +1
  - Mormont Veteran +1
  - Mormont Bruisers +1
  - Maege Mormont +2

## Final Objective: The Pack Survives
At the end of Month 6, House Stark gains the following Might based on how many Sacrifices they completed throughout the Campaign:

| Achievement | 1 Sacrifice | 2 Sacrifices | 3 Sacrifices |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| For every Challenge battle fought in the North or Riverlands | +1 Might | +2 Might | +3 Might |
| For every Stark controlled Region with unlocked Development in the North | +2 Might | +3 Might | +4 Might |
| For holding Winterfell at the end of Month 6 | +5 Might | +7 Might | +9 Might |
