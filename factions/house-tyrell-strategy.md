# House Tyrell Strategy

### Faction Overview
**Ladder of Thorns:** House Tyrell is as renowned for its wealth and prosperity as it is for the size of its vast army. Always the political climbers, the lords and ladies of Highgarden deftly maneuver into positions of power as allies of the strongest forces in Westeros. Their friends reap the benefits of money and manpower—their enemies are just another rung on the ladder to domination.

#### Invested Materiel
When House Tyrell participates in a battle, tally surviving and destroyed Combat Units from the battle:
*   **If more friendly Combat Units survived than enemy Combat Units:** Gain +1 Coin for each additional Combat Unit survived.
*   **If more friendly Combat Units were destroyed than enemy Combat Units:** Your opponents gain +1 Might for each additional Combat Unit they destroyed.

### Vassal Diplomacy & Overlord Rules
**Independent Might Rank: 4**

**Vassal Diplomacy:**
When House Tyrell becomes a Vassal, transfer all current Might to their Overlord immediately when the Overlord is declared during the Council Phase each Month. House Tyrell is not required to transfer any Might to their Overlord until the end of Month 6. Highgarden (if unlocked) remains under the control of House Tyrell. If Highgarden is Conquered by a Faction other than its Overlord, it is returned to the Overlord of House Tyrell until the end of Month 6. If House Tyrell declares independence or changes its Overlord, for all Regions in the Reach and their unlocked Bonuses (+1 Might per Region), you immediately take them (if you are now independent) or your new Overlord takes them.

**Overlord Rule:**
*   **Month 1:** House Baratheon
*   **Month 2:** Must be House Baratheon if Renly Baratheon has not been Killed / Otherwise must be any faction above Might Rank 4.
*   **Month 3:** Must be any faction above Might Rank 5 and current Coin is greater than 10 at the start of Council Phase / Otherwise must be independent.
*   **Month 4:** Must be any faction above Might Rank 4 and current Coin is greater than 15 at the start of Council Phase / Otherwise must be independent.
*   **Month 5:** Must be any faction above Might Rank 3 and current Coin is greater than 20 at the start of Council Phase / Otherwise must be independent.
*   **Month 6:** Any faction / Must not be independent.

### Special Rules
**Challenge Condition: Fertile Homeland**
When House Tyrell Commanders are put forth in a Challenge, they always gain Event (17) Construction Boon. Do not roll for this Event as having been rolled. Another faction may still roll this Event this Month.
