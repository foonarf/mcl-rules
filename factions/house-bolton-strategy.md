# House Bolton Strategy

### Faction Overview
House Bolton is feared as an especially cunning and fearsome force throughout Westeros. Each Bolton Commander generates Schemes at the end of each game to gain the amount of Might equal to the current total number of Schemes across all Commanders. Dead Commanders and Neutral Commanders do not generate Schemes. Bolton Commanders continue to generate Schemes. When a Bolton Commander Dies, his remaining Schemes may be distributed to the other Bolton Commanders. Schemes may be spent for the immediate effects listed below but only when the character is acting as a Commander. Boltons may include any Neutral Combat unit for no additional cost and are never Neutral. Other factions wishing to include Neutral Bolton units in their army must do so with the consent of the Boltons and must pay the Coin cost for including Neutral units.

### Long-Term Planning
Bolton Commanders generate the following amount of Schemes at the end of each listed Month:
*   **End of Month 1:** +2 Schemes
*   **End of Month 2:** +3 Schemes
*   **End of Month 3:** +4 Schemes
*   **End of Month 4:** +5 Schemes
*   **End of Month 5:** +5 Schemes

### Relentless Warfare
Bolton Commanders generate the following Schemes after the resolution of battles in which they participate (even when not Commanding):
*   **After a defeat:** +1 Scheme
*   **After a victory:** +2 Schemes
*   **After Capturing a foe:** +2 Schemes

### Perfect Betrayal
Bolton Commanders generate Schemes when they turn on their former allies. The first time a Commander Challenges a faction that previously fought a battle alongside the Boltons, gain +3 Schemes. This effect may be triggered only once for each Bolton Commander. Generate an additional +3 Schemes after resolving any such Challenge.
