# Martell Character Traits V1.1

## Faction Trait: House Martell Faction Trait
Serpent's Mirage - Each time this Character is on the board at the resolution of a battle, target one enemy Character who has Deadly Foe, Element of Surprise, or Frontline Warrior.  This Character gains that Trait if they do not have it for the resolution of this battle.  Each enemy Character may only be targeted by one instance of Serpent's Mirage per battle.
(Effects from Deadly Foe and Element of Surprise may stack from your Characters and gaining Frontline Warrior multiple times may cause that effect to trigger more than once per Campaign.)

## Areo Hotah
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Formation Disruption** — When Areo is chosen to participate in a battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  Declare a single army list size bonus applicable to a single unit type; it is disabled for this battle.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Arianne Martell
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** NCU
- **Escorted Princess** — If Arianne is deployed in armies that include Arys Oakeart or Daemon Sand she may ignore normal travel restrictions to participate in that battle.
- **Dornish Noble** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in Dorne, gain an additional +1 Might.

## Arys Oakheart
- **Faction:** Martell/ Lannister
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Royal Emissary** — The first time Arys Is injured for the Lannisters or as an Attachment or as part of the Kingsguard unit he is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. He does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately switches to the Martells.  This may also occur by the Lannister Strategy.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Daemon Sand
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Maneuver Disruption** — When Daemon is chosen to participate in a battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  Declare a single army list size bonus applicable to the battle Region; it is disabled for this battle.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this unit is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Doran Martell
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Orchestrated Disruption** — When Doran is Commanding and participates in a Challenge battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  Declare any single army list size bonus available to that faction, it is disabled for this battle.  The Might Rank bonus is immune to this effect.
- **Dornish Prince** — When Doran participates in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +2 Might.  If the battle took place in Dorne, gain an additional +2 Might  If the your opponents utilized any Might Rank bonus, gain an additional +2 Might..

## Edric Dayne
- **Faction:** Martell/ Brotherhood
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Adventurous Youth** — Edric begins the Campaign as a Martell character.  Each time the Martells conclude a battle against the Brotherhood Without Banners, Edric switches to ownership by the other faction.
- **Dornish Noble** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in Dorne, gain an additional +1 Might.

## Elia Sand
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Contract Disruption** — When Elia is chosen to participate in a battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  That faction may not gain Coin Bonus for this battle.  If this targets the Lannisters, their Coin Bonus is halved instead.
- **Interceptor** — When this Character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.

## Ellaria Sand
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** NCU
- **Paramour** — Unless Ellaria is named as the target of (15) Sequester Foe, any army she is included in is immune from the effects of that Event.
- **Feminine Wiles** — If this Character is still on the board at the end of the battle, choose 1 uninjured enemy Character, they must make a Capture Save with -1 to their roll.

## Gerold Dayne
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Nemesis Disruption** — When Gerold is chosen to participate in a battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  Declare a single army list size bonus applicable to a Commander; it is disabled for this battle.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this Character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.

## Harmen Uller
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Officer Disruption** — When Harmen is chosen to participate in a battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  Declare a single army list size bonus applicable to a non-named Attachment; it is disabled for this battle.
- **Dune Marshall** — When Harmen is Commanding, Securing costs no Might.  When this Character participates as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Battle Fatigue card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.

## Nymeria Sand
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Logistics Disruption** — When Nymeria is chosen to participate in a battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  Declare a single army list size bonus applicable to an NCU; it is disabled for this battle.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy Character who was out of play at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Obara Sand
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Champion Disruption** — When Obara is chosen to participate in a battle, after the factions are determined, you may choose one of your opposing factions.  Declare a single army list size bonus applicable to a named attachment (non-Commander); it is disabled for this battle.
- **Venom Collection** — When Obara is Commanding as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Poisoned Water card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck

## Oberyn Martell
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment/NCU
- **Even in Death** — If Oberyn's army wins and he was still in play at the end of the battle, choose 1 enemy Character who was not in play at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.  If Oberyn was not in play at the end of the battle, thar Character suffers -2 to their Death Save instead.
- **Master of the Duel** — Each time this Character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney or a Trial by Combat, they may re-roll one die during that Event or Trial.

## Quentyn Martell
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Seafarer** — Quentyn is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.  When participating in a Sea Battles, Quentyn's unit suffers -1 Wound from the Torrent keyword.
- **Knight of Dorne** — When this Character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in Dorne, gain an additional +1 Might.

## Tyene Sand
- **Faction:** Martell
- **Type:** NCU
- **Effective Poison** — Enemy NCUs who trigger The Strangler during battles must roll an Injury Save at the conclusion of the battle.  If The Strangler had caused that NCU to be removed from the game prior to the end (or negotiated resolution) of the battle, that NCU must make a Death Save instead.
- **Feminine Wiles** — If this Character is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, choose 1 uninjured enemy Character, they must make a Capture Save with -1 to their roll.
