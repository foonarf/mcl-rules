# House Martell - Strategy Sheet

## Faction Overview: Prince's Intrigues
Throughout the Campaign House Martell carefully monitors their adversaries and manipulates up to five different factions concurrently in the Intrigues below. Factions may only be named once.

### Emissaries From Dorne
- **Activation:** Each Month during Council Phase declare one Martell Character.
- **Rule:** Until the end of the Month, all non-Commander versions of that Character ignore location restrictions.

### Playing Into Our Hands
- **Bonus:** House Martell gains +9 Might from victories in Intercept Challenges (attacking or defending).

## Unique Mechanics: The Intrigues

### Subversion
- **Trigger:** Once per Month when this Faction gains Coin it loses -1 Coin and you gain +1 Coin.
- **Conditions & Bonuses:**
  - Gain **Spearlord +1** until this Faction defeats you in a battle, resetting this Intrigue and preventing them from being selected again.
  - If this faction is lower Might Rank than the Martells, gain **Areo Hotah +1**.
  - Gain +7 Might.
  - If this Faction improves three Might Ranks higher than their rank when they were named in this Intrigue, gain +13 Might.

### Revenge
- **Trigger:** After one of your Characters is Captured or Killed, choose a responsible faction.
- **Conditions & Bonuses:**
  - Each of the remaining listed Characters gains +1 Might when fighting this faction until they defeat you in battle, resetting this Intrigue and preventing them from being selected again.
  - If that Character was Nymeria Sand, Tyene Sand, Obara Sand, Elia Sand, or Oberyn Martell, Gain +2 Might.
  - If that Character was another was listed, gain +2 Might when fighting battles against this Faction.
  - If this Faction drops three Might Ranks from their rank when they were named in this Intrigue, gain +13 Might.

### Rivalry
- **Trigger:** Each time these Factions battle each other and you are not involved, gain +2 Might.
- **Conditions & Bonuses:**
  - This faction may not gain Commander bonuses when facing you in battle until they defeat you.
  - If the rivals battle each other a third time, choose a side to support and declare it, even if you do not participate in the battle. If you do participate in any battles between them, gain **Arianne Martell +1**, **Daemon Sand +1**, and **Arys Oakheart +1** permanently.
  - If you win a battle alongside the rival you chose to support, gain +13 Might.

### Isolation
- **Trigger:** After another Faction's Commander enters Dorne, the Sea of Dorne, or the Summer Sea.
- **Conditions & Bonuses:**
  - Gain **Gerold Dayne +1** in any battles in those Regions as long as any of that Faction's Commanders are present.
  - That Faction may not gain Might Rank bonuses in those Regions and loses -2 Might per Month until they defeat the Martells in battle, resetting this Intrigue and preventing them from being selected again.
  - Gain **Edric Dayne +1** in any battles fought outside those Regions until the trespassing Faction is expelled.
  - If that Faction is expelled from all the listed Regions because of a victory by the Martells, gain **High Hermitage Sentinel +1** and **Spearmen Captain +1** permanently.
  - Gain +7 Might.
  - Gain +13 Might.

### Ambush
- **Trigger (Left):** If this faction gains +3 or more Might from a single effect, they suffer -1 Might and you gain an additional +1 Might.
- **Trigger (Right):** After two Factions fight against each other in two battles.
- **Conditions & Bonuses:**
  - If this faction is higher Might Rank than House Martell, gain +1 Might each time they gain +2 or more Might from a single effect.
  - Gain **Doran Martell +1** and **Quentyn Martell +1**.
  - Gain **Quentyn Martell +1** and **Harmen Uller +1** until this faction defeats you in a battle, resetting this Intrigue and Preventing that faction from being selected again.
  - These Factions may not gain Commander bonuses when fighting any other factions.
  - If these Factions ally in battle against House Martell they do not gain Might Rank Bonus.
  - You gain **Dervish Captain +1** for this battle and permanently if you win.
  - If you lose or draw, reset this Intrigue and gain +7 Might.
  - House Martell may decline Team Battles against these Factions if they are allied.
