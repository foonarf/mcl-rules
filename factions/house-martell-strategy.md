# House Martell - Strategy Sheet

## EMISSARIES FROM

Each Month during Council Phase declare one Martell Character. Until the end of the Month, all non- Commander versions of that Character ignore location restrictions.

## DORNE

If you win a battle alongside the rival you chose to support, gain +13 Might.

House Martell gains +9 Might from victories in Intercept Challenges (attacking or defending).

## PLAYING INTO OUR HANDS

After one of your Characters is Captured or Killed, choose a responsible faction.

Each time these Factions battle each other and you are not involved, gain +2 Might.

If this faction is higher Might Rank than House Martell, gain +1 Might each time they gain +2 or more Might from a single effect. Gain Doran Martell +1 and Quentyn Martell +1.

If this Faction improves three Might Ranks higher than their rank when they were named in this Intrigue, gain +13 Might.

If that Character was Nymeria Sand, Tyene Sand, Obara Sand, Elia Sand, or Oberyn Martell, Gain +7 Might.

If that Faction is expelled from all the above Region because of a victory by the Martells, gain High Hermitage Sentinel +1 and Spearmen Captain +1 permanently. Gain +13 Might.

These Factions may not gain Commander bonuses when fighting any other factions.

If that Character was another was not listed, gain +2 Might when fighting battles against this Faction.

That Faction may not gain Might Rank bonuses in those Regions and loses -2 Might per Month until they defeat the Martells in battle, resetting this Intrigue and preventing them from being selected again. Gain +7 Might.

If these Factions ally in battle against House Martell they do not gain Might Rank Bonus. You gain Dervish Captain +1 for this battle and permanently if you win. If you lose or draw, reset this Intrigue and gain +7 Might.

Each of the remaining listed Characters gains +1 when fighting this faction until they defeat you in battle, resetting this Intrigue and preventing that Faction from being selected again.

Gain Edric Dayne +1 in any battles fought outside those Regions until the trespassing Faction is expelled.

Throughout the Campaign House Martell carefully monitors their adversaries and manipulates them by declaring up to five different factions concurrently in the Intrigues below. Factions may only be named in one Intrigue at a time.

Gain Gerold Dayne +1 in any of those Regions as long as any of that Faction's Commanders are present.

If the rivals battle each other a third time, choose a side to support and declare it, even if you do not participate in the battle. If you do participate in any battles between them, gain Arianne Martell+1, Daemon Sand +1, and Arys Oakhart +1 permanently.
