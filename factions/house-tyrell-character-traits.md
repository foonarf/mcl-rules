## Faction Trait: House Tyrell Faction Trait
Golden Rose Titles - Each time this Character is Injured, gain +1 Coin.  Each time this Character is Captured, gain +2 Coin.  If this Character is Killed, gain +4 Coin.  If this Character is a Commander and is still in play at its conclusion gains +2 Coin.

## Arryk
- **Faction:** Tyrell
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Twin-at-Arms** — When this character participates in a battle with another Character who has this trait and is still in play at the end of the battle, gain +1 Coin.

## Baelor Hightower
- **Faction:** Tyrell
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this Character passes a Death Save, this Character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.
- **Port Authority** — When the Baelor Hightower participates in a Sea Battle, House Hightower units suffer -1 Wound from the Torrent keyword.

## Butterbumps
- **Faction:** Tyrell
- **Type:** NCU
- **Affluent Diversion** — When Butterbumps participates a in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Coin for each NCU your opponent(s) deployed who were not Injured, Captured, or Killed at the conclusion of the battle.
- **Easily Overlooked** — This character is immune to any negative effects against their Capture Saves.

## Dickon Tarly
- **Faction:** Tyrell
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Squire of the Reach** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory in the Reach and was still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.
- **Interceptor** — When this character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.

## Garlan Tyrell
- **Faction:** Tyrell

- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this Character passes a Death Save, this Character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Gormon

That character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.
- **Maester of the Reach** — Gormon is eligible to hold the Grandmaester seat on the Small Council.  When Gormon participates in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +2 Coin.

- **Cavaliers of the Reach** — If this unit takes part in a battle outside the Reach gain +1 Might if they are still in play at its conclusion.  If this unit takes part in a battle in the Reach gain +2 Might at its conclusion.

## Leyton Hightower
- **Faction:** Tyrell
- **Type:** Commander
- **Beacon of the South** — When Leyton is Commanding as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Combat Chaos card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.
- **Counterspells** — Once per Campaign, when Leyton is Commanding, after deployment roll a d6.  One of your opponents suffers -1 Tactics Hand Size until the end of the Round number that was rolled.

## Loras Tyrell
- **Faction:** Tyrell  / Baratheon / Renly
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Rising Star** — This trait supersedes any Loras traits listed in other factions.  Loras begins the campaign as a Baratheon character and may only be deployed by the Tyrells when they are Vassal to the Baratheons.  If Renly Baratheon is Killed, Loras switches ownership to Tyrell.  When Loras participates in a battle, choose 1 Character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.
- **Highgarden Favorite** — Commander bonuses to Loras may never be cancelled for any reason.  When he participates in (20) Lavish Tourney, they may re-roll one die during that Event.

## Mace Tyrell
- **Faction:** Tyrell

- **Dignitary of Highgarden** — When Mace participates in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in any Capital and Mace was victorious, gain +2 Might and +2 Coin.
