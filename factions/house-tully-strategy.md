# House Tully - Strategy Sheet

## Faction Overview: Lands of the Lord Paramount
House Tully of the Riverlands occupies a crucial territory for both the military and economic aspirations of the other Great Houses of Westeros. As rivals make their moves, the Riverlords will have to fight for every inch of their homeland or wither in the shadow of others' ambition.

- **Independent Might Rank:** 6
- **Unforgiving Realm:** Every time House Tully participates in a battle in the Riverlands, once per battle when an enemy Character passes a Save you may force that Character to re-roll that Save.
- **Challenge Condition: Homefront Struggle:** When House Tully Commanders are put forth in a Challenge they always gain Event (6) All or Nothing. Do not count this Event as having been rolled. Another faction may trigger an Event this Month.

## Unique Mechanics: Vassalage & Diplomacy

### Vassal Diplomacy
- When House Tully becomes a Vassal, transfer half (round down) of your current Might and Coin to your Overlord immediately when the vassalage is declared during Council Phase each Month.
- If House Tully transfers Vassal to a second Overlord Faction or declares independence in a later Month, transfer up to three Regions (with all associated Developments) in the Riverlands held by your previous Overlord to your new Overlord or House Tully (if turning Independent). This can only occur once during the Campaign.
- If House Tully betrays an Overlord or abandons the fealty of their Overlord early, only one Region may be transferred in this manner and you transfer all remaining Might and Coin.

### Overlord Rule (Conditions by Month)
- **Month 1:** Independent
- **Month 2:** Must be House Stark if Sacrifice I is in effect / Otherwise may be independent
- **Month 3:** May be any Faction with 2 or more Commanders on the Campaign Map in the Riverlands / Otherwise may be independent
- **Month 4:** May be any Faction with 3 or more Commanders on the Campaign Map in the Riverlands / Otherwise may be independent
- **Month 5:** May be any Faction with the most victories in the Riverlands since the start of the Campaign / Otherwise may be independent
- **Month 6:** Any Faction / Must not be independent

## Locations and Territories: Region & Development Bonuses
The following number of Regions or Developments in the Riverlands must be held by House Tully or their Overlord to receive the listed faction-wide bonuses.

### Battleground of the Kingdoms (Regions held)
- **1 Region:** Gain +3 Might at the end of each Month
- **2 Regions:** Desmond Grell +1
- **3 Regions:** Robert Ryger +1
- **4 Regions:** Willis Wode +1
- **5 Regions:** Patrek Mallister +1
- **6 Regions:** Marq Piper +1
- **7 Regions:** Lucas Blackwood +1, Harry Rivers +1. Blackwood and Bracken units no longer have increased cost from Rivalry.

### Heartlands of the Kingdoms (Developments held)
- **1 Development:** Gain +3 Coin at the end of each Month
- **2 Developments:** Vyman +1
- **3 Developments:** Utherydes Wayn +1
- **4 Developments:** Lyman Darry +1
- **5 Developments:** Roslin Frey +1
- **6 Developments:** Rymund the Rhymer +1
- **7 Developments:** +1 Might and +1 Coin at the end of each Month. Unlock both Developments in the Trident, gaining both effects and having access to both Castles (defender's choice when the Region is attacked).

## Justice of the Cause (Brotherhood Mechanic)
At the end of each Month, the Brotherhood Without Banners must make a judgement on the cause of House Tully. The Brotherhood must declare that the cause of House Tully is **Just** or **Unjust**.

- **If Just:** Brotherhood controlled Developments in the Riverlands count toward Heartland of the Kingdoms until the end of the following Month.
- **If Unjust:** Brotherhood controlled Commanders gain +3 in battles against the cause of House Tully until the end of the following Month.
