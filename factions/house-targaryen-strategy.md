# House Targaryen - Strategy Sheet

## Faction Overview: Triumphant Return
The invasion by House Targaryen is powerful and well-funded but risks exhausting itself if aggressive momentum is not maintained. They start with no controlled Regions and must use their Commanders in Challenges or Skirmishes from adjacent Sea Regions during Month 1. Targaryens must select and declare one War Focus which provides faction-wide bonuses for the duration of the Month. Each Targaryen army deployed may utilize up to three bonuses from the Current War Focus. Up to two additional bonuses may be unlocked by controlling Regions in that Realm at the end of Month 6, gain +7 Might for each Capital controlled.

## Achievements & Objectives

### Quell the Kingdom
During Challenge Phase gain +3 Might each time the Targaryens Challenge an opposing faction they have not yet Challenged. Gain +4 Might each time the Targaryens Challenge a new Realm.

### Revealed Loyalists
For each Capital controlled by House Targaryen, gain control of one Neutral Region in the same Realm at the end of each Month. If there are no Neutral Regions in that Realm, choose one Region in that Realm controlled by the lowest possible Might Rank and take control of it, including any unlocked Developments. If held by the same faction at the end of Month 6, gain +7 Might for each Capital controlled.

### Depth of Command
Each Targaryen Commander who is on the map or in Reserve contributes bonus Might at the start of each Month. Daenerys Targaryen earns double Might from this effect.
- **Months 1 and 2:** +3 Might
- **Months 3 and 4:** +2 Might
- **Months 5 and 6:** +1 Might

## Army & Fleet Structure

### Essosi Armadas
During Challenge Phase, Targaryen Commanders may be put into Challenges (but not Skirmishes) in any eastern Sea Region without moving onto an adjacent Region. If there is a hostile Fleet in the Sea Region who does not block movement, they may be Challenged. This includes the Summer Sea.

### Exclusive Contracts
This Month you may pay -10 Coin before the Neutral Auction in Council Phase to permanently make Daario Naharis join House Targaryen. He must not be Killed, Captured, or already taken by Rising Opportunist to trigger this effect.

**Shared Bonuses:** Bonuses below may be shared with allied Commanders before the start of a battle where you both participate. If any of these bonuses are used by an ally, that faction does not have to pay the Coin cost of deploying the associated unit.
- Stormcrow Archers +1
- Stormcrow Mercenaries +1
- Stormcrow Dervishes +1
- Stormcrow Lieutenant +1
- Illyrio Mopatis +1

## Special Rules
- **Daario Naharis:** May be bid on conventionally during the Neutral auction or may be permanently acquired via Exclusive Contracts.
- **Barristan Selmy:** May be acquired upon becoming Injured or upon being released by the Lannisters at the end of Month 1.
