## Faction Trait: House Lannister Faction Trait
The Lion Doesn't Concern - Each time this Character is Injured, gain +1 Might.  Each time this Character is Captured, gain +2 Might.  If this Character is Killed, gain +4 Might.  One Character with this Trait who participates in a battle as an NCU and is still in play at its conclusion gains +2 Coin.

## Addam Marbrand
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Chivalrous Virtue** — When this Character is Commanding, Sack may not be chosen by this faction and Conquering costs no Might.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll one die while fighting in a Trial by Combat.
- **Western Outrider** — After the Lannister player rolls an Event, if Addam is available you may replace that Event with (16) Keen Scouting, then place Addam on the board for the Challenge.

## Arys Oakheart
- **Faction:** Lannister/ Martell
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Royal Emissary** — The first time Arys Is injured for the Lannisters he is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. He does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately switches to the Martells.  This may also occur by the Lannister Strategy.
- **Knight of the Stormlands** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Stormlands, gain an additional +1 Might.

## Barristan Selmy
- **Faction:** Lannister/ Targaryen
- **Type:** Attachment
- **For Honor** — The first time Barristan Is injured for the Lannisters he is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. He does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately switches to the Targaryens.  This may also occur by the Lannister Strategy.
- **Master of the Duel** — Each time this character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney or a Trial by Combat, they may re-roll one die during that Event or Trial.

## Boros Blount
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Knight of the Crownlands** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Crownlands, gain an additional +1 Might.
- **Feared by Smallfolk** — When participating in a siege battle, gain +1 Might from victories.  Gain an additional +1 Coin if Sack is chosen.

## Cersei Lannister
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** NCU
- **Manipulative Queen** — Do not track Cersei's position on the map. She may be deployed anywhere as an NCU regardless of where she appeared the previous Month. Once per Campaign when you choose to include Cersei in your army before a battle, declare one of your opposing factions in that battle.  They may not employ any Commander army list size bonuses for that battle.
- **The Curse** — If Cersei is alive, each time one of the following characters dies, gain +5 Might: Tyrion Lannister, Tywin Lannister.  If Cersei is alive, each time one of the following characters dies, gain +10 Might: Joffrey Baratheon, Tommen Baratheon.

## Gregor Clegane
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander /Attachment/ Solo
- **Monstrous Experiment** — The first time Gregor passes a Death Save as a result of being off the board at the conclusion of a battle, he is no longer Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.  If Gregor fails a Death Save, if Qyburn is not Dead or Captured, Gregor is replaced by Ser Robert Strong.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy Character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## The High Sparrow
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander / NCU
- **Public Piety** — When the High Sparrow is Commanding, enemy characters who would roll Death saves after the battle as a result of being off the board at the end of the game do not do so.  They instead roll Capture saves with -2 to their roll.  If the High Sparrow was still in play at the conclusion of a battle, gain +1 Might for every two Faith Tokens on Lannister units at the conclusion of the battle.
- **Walk of Shame** — Once per battle, when an enemy character is Captured at the end of a battle where the High Sparrow participated and was not Injured, you may instantly release that character back to its faction for +3 Might.  That character's faction suffers -3 Might.

## Jaime Lannister
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander /Attachment
- **Handless** — If Jaime is Captured, each time the Lannisters battle against the faction holding him, gain Jaime Lannister, Maimed Hostage +1 (location restrictions still apply).  If he is off the board at the end of the game as Maimed Hostage, he does not roll a Death Save and is immediately returned to the Lannisters.  Outside of these conditions, Maimed Hostage may not be deployed.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy Character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Joffrey Baratheon
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander /NCU
- **Public Executions** — When Joffrey is Commanding and is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, enemy Characters suffer -1 to Capture Saves. Choose one Character the Lannisters Captured as a result of this battle, you may immediately Kill that character and gain +3 Might.
- **Impulsive King** — Joffrey must be King of Westeros if the Lannisters hold the Red Keep.  After the Lannister player rolls an Event, if Joffrey is available you may replace that Event with (6) All or Nothing, then place Joffrey on the board for the Challenge.

## Kevan Lannister
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander /NCU
- **Westerlands Diplomat** — When Kevan participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Westerlands, gain +1 Might.  If this was a Team Battle and you were victorious, gain an additional +1 Might.
- **Prudent Marshall** — When Kevan is Commanding, any Challenge Result declared by the Lannisters and their allies costs 1 fewer Might.

## Kingsguard
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Unit
- **Joffrey's Enforcers** — Kingsguard may only include Joffrey Baratheon, Barristan Selmy, Jaime Lannister, Mandon Moore, Meryn Trant, Arys Oakheart, Boros Blount, Preston Greenfield, Sandor Clegane, or Ser Robert Strong.  If those characters are not available for play or if you choose not to include them, this unit begins the battle with Wounds indicating their absence.  Any of these characters who were removed from the tray and not restored by the end of the game must make an Injury Save.
- **Lion's Presence** — If this unit is not destroyed at the end of the battle and the Lannisters were victorious, gain +2 Might.

## Mandon Moore
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Queen's Agent** — When Mandon participates in a battle during (1) Treacherous Conditions, you may pay -2 Coin to double the effect of that Event.  If Cersei Lannister is also participating in the battle, do not pay any Coin.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Meryn Trant
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Knight of the Reach** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Reach, gain an additional +1 Might.
- **Tournament Fighter** — Each time this character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney, they may re-roll one die during that Event.

## Preston Greenfield
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Knight of the Westerlands** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Westerlands, gain an additional +1 Might.
- **Feared by Smallfolk** — When participating in a siege battle, gain +1 Might from offensive victories.  Gain an additional +1 if Sack is chosen.

## Pycelle
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** NCU
- **Questionable Remedies** — If Pycelle is still in play at the end of a battle, choose one NCU from that battle.  If an enemy NCU is chosen, they roll an Injury Save followed by a Death Save on failure.  If a friendly NCU is chosen, that NCU is immune to rolling any Death Saves after the battle.
- **Citadel's Prestige** — Whenever Pycelle participates in a battle where other maesters are present on either side, gain +1 Might for each other Maester present.  (Qyburn does not count as a maester for this Trait.)

## Qyburn
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Extreme Treatments** — If Qyburn participates in a battle where a friendly character re-rolls a Death Save due to (19) A Flesh Wound, and Qyburn was still in play at the conclusion of the battle, that character is no longer Injured.
- **Scorned by the Citadel** — Whenever Qyburn participates in a battle when no maesters are present on either side, and he is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.

## Ser Robert Strong
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Monstrous Experiment** — The first time Gregor passes a Death Save as a result of being off the board at the conclusion of a battle, he is no longer Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.  If Gregor fails a Death Save, if Qyburn is not Dead or Captured, Gregor is replaced by Ser Robert Strong.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Sandor Clegane
- **Faction:** Lannister/ Neutral
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Burned Dog** — The first time Sandor is injured for the Lannisters unit he is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. He does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately becomes a Neutral Character. If re-hired by the Lannisters, he may be deployed as his Neutral or Lannister version.  This may also occur by the Lannister Strategy.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Tommen Baratheon
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** NCU
- **Breaking Point** — If Joffrey Baratheon is killed and Tommen is alive, gain +5 Might. Each Council Phase that Tommen lives after that, he must roll a Death Save.
- **Timid Heir** — When Tommen participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +3 Might.  Tommen suffers -1 to all Saves.

## Tyrion Lannister
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment/NCU
- **Promises of Coin** — Whenever Tyrion is present in a battle, one unit of Stone Crows may be deployed for no Coin cost and, if he is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +2 Coin.  When Tyrion is Commanding as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Combat Chaos card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.

## Tywin Lannister
- **Faction:** Lannister
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Lion's Pride** — Each time Tywin is Commanding against a faction which has previously defeated the Lannisters, you may pay 1-3 Coin before creating your army list.  For each Coin spent, gain Tywin Lannister +1.  If Tywin is victorious and still in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might for each Coin spent on this battle through this Trait.
- **Patient Warden** — When Tywin is Commanding as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Starve Them Out card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck.
