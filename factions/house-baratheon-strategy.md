# House Baratheon - Strategy Sheet

## Faction Overview
House Baratheon forces are divided in their Loyalty between Stannis and Renly. Keep a current tally of the Legitimacy score of each brother and reward them with the bonuses from the side columns that meet the requirements. Reward the leading brother with the rewards from the center column. If the scores are tied, both brothers gain the bonuses in the center column. If no Renly Commanders participate in a Challenge battle this Month, lose -8 Might. If no Stannis Commanders participate in a Challenge battle this Month, lose -8 Might.

## Loyalty & Legitimacy Mechanics
Legitimacy is tracked monthly across a 6x6 grid for both Renly and Stannis.

### Legitimacy Score Sources
- **Declared Realms:** x10 Legitimacy per realm.
- **Sworn Houses:** x3 Legitimacy per house. Baratheon controlled Regions, purchased Developments, and Declared Realm count as Sworn Houses (x3 each) and must match the same brother as the Declared Realm they are in.
- **Loyal Commanders:** x2 Legitimacy for each Baratheon Commander, as long as they are not Captured or Killed.
- **Victories:**
  - Close Victory: +1 Legitimacy
  - Decisive Victory: +2 Legitimacy
  - Total Victory: +3 Legitimacy

### Legitimacy Rewards (Tiers)

**0-34 (Insignificant Claimant):** May only use Loyalty units and Attachments.

**35-49 (Weak Claimant):**
- Greater Claim - Renly: Highgarden Pikemen +1
- Greater Claim - Stannis: Dragonstone Noble +1
- Both: Baratheon Wardens +1, Baratheon Sentinels +1, Master Warden +1

**50-64 (Strong Claimant):**
- Greater Claim - Renly: Thorn Watch Sentinel +1, Thorn Watch +1
- Greater Claim - Stannis: Red Priestess +1, King's Men +1
- Both: Stag Knights +1, Champions of the Stag +1, Stag Knight Noble +1

**65-79 (Irrefutable Claimant):**
- Greater Claim - Renly: Stormcrows +1, Riders of Highgarden +1
- Greater Claim - Stannis: R'hllor Queen's Men +1, R'hllor Lightbringers +1
- Both: Crownland Scouts +1, Alester Florent +1, Shyra Errol +1

**80+ (Destined for the Throne):**
- Greater Claim - Renly: Renly Loyalty Named Attachment +1, Renly Loyalty NCU +1
- Greater Claim - Stannis: Stannis Loyalty Named Attachment +1, Stannis Loyalty NCU +1
- Both: Baratheon Commanders +2

## Declared Realms
- Announce Declared Realms for either brother (x10 Legitimacy each) during Council Phase as long as they contain at least one Baratheon controlled Region.
- Realms may change Loyalty between brothers during Council Phase.
- If any Baratheon Commander moves into or through a Declared Realm which does not match his Loyalty, he is automatically Injured at the start of the next Council Phase.

## Internal Affairs
Either brother may be Killed by the other during Council Phase of Month 1. If one brother kills the other, he gains a permanent +20 Legitimacy (logged as Commander Loyalty) to remain in effect. If a Commander is executed for violations of Law, the Baratheon player must keep the Commander card for the brother he fought under.

## Special Rules and Martial Traditions

### Knightly Orders (Renly)
- When Renly Loyalty units gain Veterancy, roll twice on the Veterancy table and choose the result.
- If Renly has the Greater Claim at the end of Month 6, gain +1 Might for each Veterancy bonus applied to surviving Renly Loyalty Units.

### Martial Traditions (Stannis)
- If two Stannis Loyalty units (including NCUs) are destroyed during a battle, when you award Veterancy, award two effects to that Veteran unit.
- If Stannis has the Greater Claim at the end of Month 6, gain +1 Might for every Dead Stannis Loyalty Character.
