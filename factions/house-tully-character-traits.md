## Faction Trait: House Tully Faction Trait
Duty of Sacrifice - If this Character fails any Save, choose one other friendly Character, they may re-roll one Save of any type for this battle.  If this Character participates in a battle as an NCU, gain +1 Might or +1 Coin.

## Blackfish's Riders
- **Faction:** Tully
- **Type:** Unit
- **Handpicked Men** — Record how many Wounds this unit has sustained at the end of the battle.  If this unit participates in a battle next month, it begins that battle with the name number of suffered Wounds.
- **Heroes of the Riverlands** — If this unit takes part in a battle outside the Riverlands gain +1 Might.  If this unit takes part in a battle in the Riverlands gain +2 Might.

## Brynden Tully
- **Faction:** Tully  /
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Brother in Exile** — This trait supersedes any Brynden traits listed in other Factions.  Brynden begins the campaign as an Arryn character and switches ownership to Tully in Month 2.  Brynden is then considered a Tully character but may be deployed as a Stark character if the Tullys are Vassal to the Starks and the Tullys consent.  When Brynden is Commanding he may pass through 1 Region of impassible terrain during Challenge Phase.
- **Honed Veteran** — Whenever Brynden is deployed in an army, you may cancel the enemy's effect of (16) Keen Scouting.  When Brynden is Commanding  in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Combat Chaos card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.

## Edmure Tully
- **Faction:** Tully
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Headstrong Heir** — During Challenge Phase if Edmure is not Captured or Killed and you put forth any Tully Commander in a Challenge outside the Riverlands, gain Commander +2 or Edmure Tully +3 for that battle.  Declare immediately.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this character's army wins and this character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Desmond Grell
- **Faction:** Tully
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Knight of the Riverlands** — When this Character participates as an Attachment in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Riverlands, gain an additional +1 Might.
- **Dispatch Rivermen** — When Desmond participates in a battle you count as controlling one additional Region for Battleground of the Kingdoms.  If you already have all seven effects, gain Desmond Grell +1.
