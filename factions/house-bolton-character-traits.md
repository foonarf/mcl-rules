# Bolton Character Traits V1.1

## Faction Trait: House Bolton Faction Trait
Bloody Mischief - For every three Characters with this Trait still in play at the end of a battle, choose one enemy Character still in play from that battle.  They suffer a Capture Save with -2 to their roll.  When participating in a battle as an NCU, this unit is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Ben Bones
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Loose the Bloodhounds** — When Ben participates as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Surprise Sortie card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.
- **Easily Overlooked** — This character is immune to any negative effects against their Capture Saves.

## Damon Dance-For-Me
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Tournament Fighter** — Each time this character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney, they may re-roll one die during that Event.

## Grunt
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Weeping Water Brute** — When participating in a Sea Battles, Grunt's unit suffers -1 Wound from the Torrent keyword.

## Jeyne Poole
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** NCU
- **Questionable Allegiance** — When Jenye participates in a battle, and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might. If you were defeated, Jenye suffers -1 to Capture Saves.
- **Feminine Wiles** — If this character is still on the board at the end of the battle, choose 1 uninjured enemy character, they must make a Capture Save with -1 to their roll.

## Ramsay Snow
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment/NCU
- **Indiscriminate Malice** — If Ramsay participates as an NCU and is still in play at the conclusion of a battle and an enemy character must make an Injury or Death Save as a result of that battle: choose one enemy NCU who participated in that battle, that character must make an Injury Save with -1 to their roll instead, and the original character automatically passes their Injury or Death Save.  Ramsay may be played as his Neutral version when Commanding or deployed with a Neutral Commander.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.

## Roose Bolton
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Devious Surprise** — if Roose is put forth in a Challenge and an opposing Commander is from a faction with which you previously fought a Team Battle as an ally, you may replace any Event that you rolled with (8) Lying in Wait.  Roose may be played as his Neutral version when Commanding or deployed with a Neutral Commander.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Skinner
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Risky Intimidation** — Each time Skinner participates in a battle and Is still in play at its conclusion, choose one enemy character.  They suffer -1 to their roll on a single Injury or Capture Save.  If that character passes their roll with a 6, Skinner permanently loses this ability.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Sour Alyn
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Interceptor** — When this character participates in an Intercept battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.

## Steelshanks Walton
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Undeterred Soldier** — When Steelshanks is Commanding as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Sapper's Tunnel card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck.  When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), Steelshanks is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Theon Greyjoy / Reek
- **Faction:** Bolton / Greyjoy
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Wayward Son** — The first time Theon Greyjoy is injured for the Greyjoy Player he does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately becomes Theon Greyjoy - Reek (controlled by the Boltons).  He may not be fielded again by the Greyjoys until he has been fielded by the Boltons and is absent from the board at the end of the game.
- **Tortured Ward** — "Reek" is not considered a unique character unless Wayward Son is in effect.  Theon Greyjoy must be deployed if Ramsay Snow is Commanding in battle against the Greyjoys.

## Tybald
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** NCU
- **Experienced Healer** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  That character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.
- **Control the Ravens** — During Council phase if Tybald participated in a battle the previous Month in a Region the Boltons now own, you may perform Intercepts anywhere in that Realm.  Your Commander may not be Intercepted on his way to this Challenge.

## Walda Frey
- **Faction:** Bolton
- **Type:** NCU
- **Silver Dowry** — Walda always gains +2 Coin for participating in a battle and, as long as she has not been Killed, the Boltons may on Walder Frey with a +2 Coin equivalent credit, ignoring the effects of Questionable Allegiance.
- **Marriage Pact** — When Walda participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the North or the Riverlands and you were victorious, gain +2 Might.
