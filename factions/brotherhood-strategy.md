# Brotherhood Without Banners Strategy

### Faction Overview
The Brotherhood Without Banners attempts to carry out their final dispatch from dead King Robert Baratheon's throne and bring justice to the Seven Kingdoms. Justice is counted immediately when it is earned and grants the faction-wide bonuses listed in the King's Justice track.

### Unique Mechanics

#### Refuge Among Commoners
*   The Brotherhood may never take control of any Region and may never hold control of Developments by Conquering or Routing.
*   There is no Coin cost when the Brotherhood takes control of a Development.
*   Brotherhood control of Developments persists even if the attached Region is controlled by another faction and may only be removed if a faction Challenging the Region specifically declares that they are attacking the Brotherhood Development. In such cases the Brotherhood may use any Castles tied to that Development.

#### Unconventional Warfare
*   Opposing factions do not gain Might Rank bonuses in 1v1 battles against the Brotherhood.
*   In Team Battles, the highest Might Rank opponent counts as having the same Might Rank as the Brotherhood.
*   If the Brotherhood has not yet acquired Catelyn Stark, they may bid on one Neutral Commander with a +3 Coin equivalent credit. (The credit remains available if you are outbid).

#### Trial by Combat
Once per Month during Council Phase the Brotherhood may declare a Trial by Combat for any Captured character held by any faction. 
*   Involved factions may not negotiate release of the character until the Trial is resolved. 
*   The Captive's faction and the faction holding the Captive each choose one Champion character who is available to be present in a Region chosen by the faction holding the captive. 
*   Champions may not be intercepted on their way to the Trial. 
*   The Captive may serve as their own Champion and either Champion may be a character from allied factions. 
*   The Brotherhood must declare one of the Champions to be Favored. 
*   Champion factions each roll a d6 and add modifiers:
    *   Champion is Favored: +1 to your roll
    *   Champion is a Brotherhood Character: +1 to your roll
    *   Champion is a Commander: +2 to your roll **OR** Champion is a 2-point Attachment: +1 to your roll
*   If the Captive's Champion wins, the Captive is released and the opposing Champion makes a Death Save -2. 
*   If the Champion of the faction holding the Captive wins or ties, the Captive is executed and their Champion (if different) makes a Death Save -2.

#### Light of R'hllor
At the end of Month 6, gain an amount of Might equal to your total current Justice.

### King's Justice (Progression Track)
Justice is earned through specific actions and unlocks cumulative bonuses.

**Earning Justice:**
*   **Any Brotherhood character survives a battle:** +1 Justice
*   **A Trial by Combat is initiated:** +4 Justice
*   **A favored Champion wins a Trial by Combat:** +5 Justice
*   **The Brotherhood wins a battle:** +7 Justice
*   **The Brotherhood fights in a Crucial Battle:** +8 Justice
*   **Controlling Developments (Once per listed place per game):**
    *   King's Landing
    *   Winterfell
    *   The Wall (Regions)
    *   Storm's End
    *   The Eyrie
    *   Pyke
    *   Sunspear
*   **One of The Accused is Injured, Captured, or Killed (+10 Justice):**
    *   *The Brotherhood must participate in a battle which causes this condition or cause it by another Brotherhood effect. Each listed character may trigger this effect once.*
    *   Gregor Clegane
    *   Rickard Karstark
    *   Ramsay Snow
    *   Mance Rayder
    *   Alliser Thorne
    *   Stannis Baratheon
    *   Jorah Mormont
    *   Euron Greyjoy
    *   Gerold Dayne

**Justice Rewards (Unlocks):**
*   **Level 1:** Reroll all failed Death Saves once.
*   **Level 2:** Brotherhood Commanders +2; Gain one Development in Dorne at the end of this Month*.
*   **Level 3:** Brotherhood characters who pass Capture Saves are no longer Injured.
*   **Level 4:** Sworn Knights +1; Gain one Development in the Reach at the end of this Month*.
*   **Level 5:** Men-at-Arms +1; Gain one Development in the North at the end of this Month*; Gain one Development in the Vale at the end of this Month*; Gain one Development in the Westerlands at the end of this Month*.
*   **Level 6:** Brotherhood Archers +1; Gain one Development in the Stormlands at the end of this Month*.
*   **Level 7:** Peasant Levy +1; Gain one Development in the Crownlands at the end of this Month*; The first Neutral Combat unit included in each Brotherhood army costs no Coin.
*   **Level 8:** Brotherhood Commanders +1; Gain one Development in the Riverlands at the end of this Month*.
*   **Level 9:** Brotherhood NCUs +1.
*   **Level 10:** Brotherhood Attachments +1.

*\*Note: The Brotherhood may never hold Developments in the Wall or Pyke and cannot use the King's Justice track to take control of any Capital Developments or the Red Keep.*
