# Baratheon Character Traits

## Davos Seaworth
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm./Att./NCU
- **Element of Surprise** — If this character's army wins and this character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.
- **Smuggler's Course** — Davos is never bound by location restrictions and may never be Intercepted in Sea Regions.

## Axell Florent
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm./Att./NCU
- **Firebrand for Stannis** — Whenever a Realm is counted for Stannis under Declared Realms, Axell generates +3 Legitimacy for Stannis if he is on the board as a Commander in that Realm in a Region not controlled by Baratheons.  (Add this value to the Legitimacy tracker under Declared Realms and remove it when these conditions are not met.)
- **Reach Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Reach, gain +2 Might.

## Cortnay Penrose
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm./Att./NCU
- **Rigorous Discipline** — When this character participates in a battle, friendly characters are immune to the effects of (2) Drunken Revelry.
- **Field Marshall** — When this character is Commanding, Securing costs -1 Might.

## Loras Tyrell
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm./Att.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this character's army wins and this character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.
- **Tournament Fighter** — Each time this character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney, they may re-roll one die during that Event.

## Justin Massey
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm./Att.
- **Knight of the Crownlands** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Crownlands and was victorious, gain +2 Might.
- **Chivalrous Virtue** — When this character is Commanding, Sack may not be chosen by this faction. Conquer costs 1 fewer Might.

## Andrew Estermont
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm./Att.
- **Rigorous Discipline** — When this character participates in a battle, friendly characters are immune to the effects of (2) Drunken Revelry
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Eldon Estermont
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm./NCU
- **Stalwart of Renly** — Whenever a Realm is counted for Renly under Declared Realms,  Eldon generates +3 Legitimacy for Renly whenever he is on the board as a Commander in a Baratheon-controlled Region in that Realm.  (Add this value to the Legitimacy tracker under Sworn Houses and remove it when these conditions are not met.)
- **Stormlands Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Stormlands, gain +2 Might.

## Stannis Baratheon
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm.
- **Righteous Cause** — When Stannis participates in a battle that battle always generates 3 Legitimacy regardless of the outcome.  (Add this value to the Legitimacy tracker under Victories in place of whatever value would have been generated by victory in that battle.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this character's army wins and this character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Renly Baratheon
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Comm.
- **Summer King** — If Renly is alive and not captured all Really Loyalty characters generate 3 Legitimacy from participating in (20) Lavish Tourney.  (Add this value on the Legitimacy tracker under Victories.)
- **Chivalrous Virtue** — When this character is Commanding, Sack may not be chosen by this faction. Conquer costs 1 fewer Might.

## Brienne
- **Faction:** Baratheon / Neutral
- **Type:** Att.
- **Renly's Sworn Sword** — As long as Renly Baratheon is alive Brienne remains a Baratheon character and may be played as Baratheon or Neutral in Baratheon armies.  The Baratheon player may also permit her use as Neutral in any friendly faction requesting her use.  If Renly is ever Killed or Captured, Brienne immediately becomes a Neutral character and may only be hired through the Neutral character pool (although she may still be played as her Baratheon versions when in service to the Baratheons).
- **Master of the Duel** — Each time this character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney or a Trial by Combat, they may re-roll one die during that Event or Trial.

- **Squire of the Stormlands** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a battle in the Stormlands and was victorious, gain +1 Might.

## Shyra Errol
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** NCU
- **Lending Legitimacy** — When Shyra is chosen to participate in a battle she doubles the Legitimacy of her Commander (adding +2 Legitimacy under Loyal Commanders).  When Shyra is chosen to participate in a later battle for any other Commander, the bonus transfers to that Commander's Loyalty.  If Shyra is Injured, Captured, or Killed, remove this bonus.
- **Stormlands Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Stormlands, gain +2 Might.

## Melisandre
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** NCU
- **Shadow Assassins** — When Stannis Baratheon and Melisandre are available, after rolling an Event, you may replace that Event with (15) Sequester Foe, then place Stannis on the board for the Challenge.  Melisandre must be played in this battle and may not be targeted by (15) Sequester Foe.  Your opponent may choose to include their character targeted by the Event but must make a Death Save -2 for that character immediately.
- **Feminine Wiles** — If this character is still on the board at the end of the battle, choose 1 uninjured enemy character, they must make a Capture Save with -1 to their roll.

## Selyse & Shireen
- **Faction:** Baratheon

- **Noble Ladies** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Reach or the Crownlands, gain +2 Might.
- Royal Family -If Selyse & Shireen are Captured, Stannis suffers -10 Legitimacy.  If Selyse & Shireen are killed, Stannis gains +10 Legitimacy.  Log these values under Sworn Houses.

## Margaery Tyrell
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** NCU
- **Experienced Healer** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  The character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.
- **Reach Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Reach, gain +2 Might.

## Olenna Tyrell
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** NCU
- **It Was Me** — When Olenna participates in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, choose one enemy NCU.  If that NCU was not Injured as a result of the battle they must make an Injury Save and upon failure, a Death Save with -1 to their roll.
- **Reach Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place in the Reach, gain +2 Might.

## Cressen
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** NCU
- **Experienced Healer** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is uninjured at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  The character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.
- **Mortal Risk** — If Cressen was destroyed by his ability during the battle he rolls an Injury Save after the battle and, on failure, a Death Save.  If he passes his Injury Save, choose 1 enemy NCU who participated in the battle.  They must make an Injury Save and, on failure, a Death Dave.

## Patchface
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** NCU
- **Stormproof** — Once per campaign when the Baratheon player rolls (3) Destructive Stormfront, if Patchface is alive, they may re-roll for that event.
- **Nimble as a Monkey** — Once per campaign Patchface may re-roll a Death or Capture Save.

## Rainbow Guard
- **Faction:** Baratheon
- **Type:** Unit
- **Renly's Retinue** — Rainbow Guard may only include Renly Baratheon, Loras Tyrell, Bryce Caron, Brienne, Parmen Crane, Emmon Cuy, Robar Royce, or Guyard Morrigen.  If those characters are not available for play or if you choose not to include them, this unit begins the battle with Wounds indicating their absence.  Any of these characters who were removed from the tray and not restored by the end of the game must make an Injury Save.
- **Favored Knights** — If this unit was not destroyed at the end of the Battle and the Baratheons were victorious, Renly gains +5 Legitimacy for this victory.
