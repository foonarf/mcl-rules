# Night's Watch Strategy

### Faction Overview

The Night's Watch are sworn to the protection of the Seven Kingdoms and serve at the Wall until death. Of the original nineteen castles that garrisoned: Castle Black, Eastwatch-by-the-Sea and the Shadow Tower. The Night's Watch must seek to restore the Abandoned Castles along the Wall. Abandoned Castles are purchased for -1 Might rather than -1 Coin. Each faction (including the Night's Watch) may contribute -1 Might from its own respective total to help the Night's Watch restore one Abandoned Castle per Month. Abandoned Castles may be restored in any Region which the Night's Watch still holds. The Night's Watch may negotiate for their need for contributions through Coin, promises of aid in battle, or any other diplomatic means. When a Wall Region is Sacked, the Abandoned Castle in that Region, it is no longer restored. If the Night's Watch loses control of any of the three Regional Castles, they temporarily lose all bonuses from Abandoned Castles until they regain control of the Region. While held, all list size bonuses from Abandoned Castles are useable in every Region of the Campaign.

### Unique Mechanics

#### The Wall
The Wall consists of 19 castles. Three are primary Regional Castles, and 16 are Abandoned Castles that provide specific bonuses when restored.

**The Shadow Tower (Regional Castle)**
*   **Bonus:** If all Abandoned Castles around The Shadow Tower are restored, each time a Night's Watch unit gains Veterancy it may roll twice on the Veterancy table and take both results, rerolling if a repeat number is rolled.

**Castle Black (Regional Castle)**
*   **Bonus:** If all Abandoned Castles around Castle Black are restored, each time you defeat an enemy in battle gain an amount of Might equal to their Might Rank bonus (or their team Might Rank bonus) from that battle. If their Might Rank bonus was reduced by a special effect, disregard that reduction.

**Eastwatch-by-the-Sea (Regional Castle)**
*   **Bonus:** If all Abandoned Castles around Eastwatch-by-the-Sea are restored, gain +1 Coin equal to the combined range of every Region controlled by the Night's Watch at the start of every Month.

#### Banished to the Wall
Enemy Characters who are taken under Night's Watch control using the "Take the Black" Tactics card and are still on the board at the conclusion of a battle must make a Capture Save with -4 to their roll even if the Night's Watch was defeated.

#### The Great Ranging
Once per Campaign you may deploy an army with all unlocked bonuses from the Wall in a Region outside the Wall Realm. This army may include two Commander Characters in their Attachment versions (one acts as the army's normal Commander). Declare this when your army list is submitted.

### Special Rules: Protection of the Realm
At the end of Month 6, the Night's Watch gain the following Might based on how well the Seven Kingdoms were protected:
*   **+5 Might** for each Challenge battle fought during the Campaign in the Wall Realm.
*   **+3 Might** for each Challenge battle fought during the Campaign.
*   **+6 Might** for each Region in the Wall Realm (including the Gift) held at the end of Month 6.
*   **+1 Might** for each defensive victory during the Campaign.
*   **+3 Might** for each restored Abandoned Castle in Regions held by the Night's Watch at the end of Month 6.
