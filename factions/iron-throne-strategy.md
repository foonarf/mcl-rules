# Iron Throne Strategy

### Faction Overview
The faction controlling the **Red Keep Development** gains access to the Iron Throne Strategy.

*   **Council Phase Bidding:** When a Neutral Character is up for appointment, this faction may enhance their bid with the equivalent value of **+2 Coin** by promising that Neutral Character a council seat (this reduces the Coin needed to win the bid and complete the appointment).
*   **Death & Capture:**
    *   Characters who die while on the Small Council may have their seat replaced by the Iron Throne faction for **no cost**.
    *   Characters captured while holding a seat retain the seat but lose their vote during Council Phase; the seat may be reappointed for normal cost at that time.
*   **Default Control:** Each seat begins the Campaign with a default character. If **Petyr Baelish** and **Varys** are not bid on by other factions, they are automatically controlled by the Iron Throne faction.
*   **Nullification:** If Kings Landing is Conquered or Sacked, the Iron Throne Strategy is nullified.

### Unique Mechanics: The Small Council
Appointments to the Small Council cost **-1 Coin** unless they are made as a result of betrayal or Character Death.

**Betrayal Rule:**
If any Character's faction participates in a betrayal of the Iron Throne Faction (opposing them in battle during Challenge Phase), their council seat may be immediately reappointed by the Iron Throne as soon as that Challenge or Skirmish is logged on the Challenge Log. This effect is not triggered if the Iron Throne faction chooses to initiate a battle against the Character's faction.

### Council Seats and Bonuses

#### King or Queen of the Seven Kingdoms
*   **If Character is:** Joffrey Baratheon, Tommen Baratheon, Cersei Lannister, Eddard Stark, Stannis Baratheon, Renly Baratheon, Jon Snow, Daenerys Targaryen, Balon Greyjoy, Euron Greyjoy, Victarion Greyjoy, Quentyn Martell, Mace Tyrell, Robb Stark, or Roose Bolton: **Gain +10 Might at the end of the Month.**
*   **If any other Commander:** **Gain +7 Might at the end of the Month.**
*   **If any other Character:** **Gain +5 Might at the end of the Month.**

#### Hand of the King
*   **Requirement:** Must be a Commander.
*   **Eligibility:** Neutrals Characters are not eligible.
*   **Combat Modifier:** Enemies do not gain Might Rank Bonus when this Character is Commanding.
*   **Bonus:** At the end of the Month **Gain +5 Might** (Character's faction).

#### Grandmaester
*   **Eligible Characters:** Pycelle, Qyburn, Cressen, Aemon, Samwell Tarly, Wendamyr, Tybald.
*   **Bonus:** Reroll 1 failed Death Save per Month (Character's faction chooses the Save).

#### Master of Coin
*   **Requirement:** Must be an NCU.
*   **Territory Bonus:** King's Landing gains **+3 Coin** (Iron Throne faction).
*   **Global Bonus:** All other Capitals gain **+2 Coin** (character's faction).

#### Lord Commander of the Kingsguard
*   **Requirement:** Must be an Attachment or Commander.
*   **Veterancy Bonus:** When attached to a unit in a Challenge or Skirmish battle for their faction, that unit gains Veterancy **before** deployment, rather than at the end of the battle.
*   **Veterancy Bonus:** When this Character's unit gains Veterancy, that unit may roll twice on the Veterancy table (re-rolling if the same number is rolled) and choose the preferred result.

#### Master of Laws
*   **Bonus:** When this Character participates in a battle, gain **+1 Tactics Hand Size** if their faction has no other source of hand size increase at the start of Round 1.

#### Master of Ships
*   **Requirement:** Must be a Commander.
*   **Blockade Ability:** During Challenge Phase, this Character may Challenge a Sea Region to set up a Blockade of an adjacent Port.
    *   **Blockade Effect:** Removes all Might and Coin income from Port Development Perks and diverts those bonuses to the Iron Throne faction.
    *   **Countering:** Blockades may be countered, broken, or defended.
    *   **Removal:** The Blockade's effect is removed if the Character is defeated in battle or stripped of the title.
*   **Movement Bonus:** When starting a Challenge Phase at sea, this Commander may move up to **two Sea Regions away** when performing a Challenge.

#### Master of Whispers
*   **Bonus:** This character's faction rolls twice for Events (re-rolling if the same number is rolled) and chooses the preferred result.
