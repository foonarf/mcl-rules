Greyjoy Character Traits

## Faction Trait: House Greyjoy Faction Trait
Kraken Tradition - If this Character participates in any battle as part of an Iron Fleet, log their location as the Sea Region location of that Iron Fleet.  If this Character is a Fleet Captain they may traverse 2 Sea Regions when Challenging and they always return to their Iron Fleet location after being deployed as an NCU or Attachment.

## Aeron Greyjoy
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** NCU
- **Ironborn Lineage** — When Aeron participates in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If this was a Sea Battle, gain an additional +1 Might and +1 Coin.
- **Ritual Drowning** — Once per campaign when a Greyjoy Character fails a Death Save, if this character is Alive and Uninjured, that Character may re-roll their Death Save.

## Asha Greyjoy
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment/ NCU
- **Kingsmoot Claimant** — When this Character was Commanding and is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might and +1 Coin for every other Character with this Trait who was still in play at the conclusion of the battle.
- **Plunderer** — When this Character is Commanding, Sacking costs no Might and gains +2 additional Coin.

## Baelor Blacktyde
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Ironborn Lord** — When Baelor participates in a victory and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +2 Might.  If this was at Sea or in the Iron Islands, gain an additional +2 Might.
- **Chivalrous Virtue** — When this character is Commanding, Sack may not be chosen by this faction and Conquering costs no Might.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll one die while fighting in a Trial by Combat.

## Balon Greyjoy
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Seastone Chair** — When Balon is acting as Fleet Captain his Iron Fleet may add one Support per Month for no Coin cost.  When Balon is Commanding in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +4 Might if your opponent(s) had Might Rank bonus, even if that bonus was removed by an effect.
- **Plunderer** — When this character is Commanding, Sacking costs no Might and gains +2 additional Coin.

## Beron Blacktyde
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** NCU
- **Ritual Drowning** — Once per campaign when a Greyjoy Character fails a Death Save, if this character is Alive and Uninjured, that Character may re-roll their Death Save.
- **Rigorous Discipline** — When this character participates in a battle, friendly Characters are immune to the effects of (2) Drunken Revelry.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll a Capture Save.

## Dagmer Cleftjaw
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Interceptor** — When this character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.
- **Not the Old Way** — When Dagmer is Commanding as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Loot and Plunder card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck.

## Erik Ironmaker
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Kingsmoot Claimant** — When this Character was Commanding and is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might and +1 Coin for every other Character with this Trait who participated in this battle.
- **Ironmade Marshall** — When Erik is Commanding, Securing costs no Might.  When Erik is Commanding as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Murder Hole card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.

## Euron Greyjoy
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Kingsmoot Claimant** — When this Character was Commanding and is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might and +1 Coin for every other Character with this Trait who was still in play at the conclusion of the battle.
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.

## Moqorro
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Vision of the Future** — Once per Campaign when Moqorro participates in a battle attached to an Iron Fleet, declare one unclaimed Vendetta at the start of that battle. That Iron Fleet gains the effect of that Vendetta for the duration of the battle.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Nute
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Fair-Weather First Mate** — When Nute participates in a battle with Victarion and both characters are still in play at the conclusion, gain +1 Might.  Once per campaign when Nute participates in a battle without Victarion, gain +2 Might.  This Trait is then disabled for the rest of the campaign.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this Character passes a Death Save, this Character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Qarl
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Tournament Fighter** — Each time this Character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney, they may re-roll one die during that Event.

## Rodrik Harlaw
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** NCU
- **Ironborn Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle took place at Sea or the Iron Islands, gain +1 Might.
- **Trade Manifests** — When this character participates in a  battle in a Sea Region or coastal Region, gain +1 Coin. On a victory, gain an additional +1 Coin.

## Theon Greyjoy
- **Faction:** Greyjoy / Bolton
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Wayward Son** — When Theon is Commanding in a land Region, enemies do not gain Might Rank bonus against him.  The first time Theon Greyjoy is injured for the Greyjoy Player he does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately becomes Theon Greyjoy - Reek (controlled by the Boltons).  He may not be fielded again by the Greyjoys until he has been fielded by the Boltons and is absent from the board at the end of the game.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still in play at the end of the battle, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.

## Tristifer Botley
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** NCU
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Capable Stewardship** — When this Character participates as an NCU and still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Coin.

## Victarion Greyjoy
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Kingsmoot Claimant** — When this Character was Commanding and is still in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might and +1 Coin for every other Character with this Trait who was still in play at the conclusion of the battle.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this Character passes a Death Save, this Character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Wendamyr
- **Faction:** Greyjoy
- **Type:** NCU
- **Experienced Healer** — When this Character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly Character.  The character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.
- **Missive from Pyke** — During Council Phase, if Wendamyr participated in a battle attached to an Iron Fleet the previous Month and was not Injured, Killed, or Captured, that Iron Fleet may add 1 available Support for no Coin cost.
