HOUSE TARGARYEN TRAITS V1.1

## Faction Trait: House Targaryen Faction Trait
Concentrated Dragonfire - If two Commanders with this Trait both put forth Challenges or Skirmishes in the same Realm, they may each reroll one Death Save as their Commander or Attachment versions this Month.  If this Character participates in a battle as an NCU and is not Injured, Captured, or Killed at its conclusion, gain +1 Coin for each Region that was held in this Realm before the battle.

## Barristan Selmy
- **Faction:** Targaryen/ Lannister
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Icon of Chivalry** — When Barristan is Commanding as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Discussing Terms card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.  When Barristan is Commanding any battle, Sack may not be chosen and Conquering costs no Might.  Once per Campaign Barristan may re-roll one die while fighting in a Trial by Combat.
- **For Honor** — The first time Barristan Is injured for the Lannisters he is not eligible to avoid the Injury in any way. He does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately switches to the Targaryens.  This may also occur by the Lannister Strategy.

## Belwas
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Deadly Foe** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy Character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.
- **Master of the Duel** — Each time this character participates in (20) Lavish Tourney or a Trial by Combat, they may re-roll one die during that Event or Trial.

## Cohollo
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Arakh Focus** — If Cohollo participates in the battle, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save. If Field Warfare is in effect, they suffer -2 to their Death Save instead.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Daenerys Targaryen
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Dragon's Focus** — When Daenerys participates as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Sow Discord card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck.  If Dragon Dominance is in effect, Daenerys is still in play at the conclusion of any battle, enemy characters suffer -1 to Death Saves.
- **Band of Heroes** — Daenerys always ignores travel restrictions unless all 3 Dragons are Dead.  After the Targaryen player rolls an Event, if Daenerys is available you may replace that Event with (10) Plentiful Leadership, then place any two available Commanders on the board for the Challenge.

## Daario Naharis
- **Faction:** Targaryen/ Neutral
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Traveling Mercenary** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), Daario is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.  If Exclusive Contracts is in effect, choose 1 enemy Character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.
- **Love Over Coin** — Stormcrow units have no Coin cost in this army or your ally's army in a Team Battle.  Daario may be permanently added to the Targaryens by their Strategy.

## Dragons (Drogon, Viserion, Rhaegal)
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Unit
- **Mythic Beasts** — Dragons are always entitled to Injury Saves if they were off the board at the conclusion of a battle.  Dragons may never be Captured.
- **Power of Valyria** — If Dragon's Dominance is in effect, dragons are immune to negative modifiers on Death Saves.

## Drogo's Bloodriders
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Unit
- **Head of the Horde** — If this unit was not destroyed at the end of an offensive Battle and the Targaryens were victorious in a developed Region, you may choose both Sack and Conquer to resolve this battle, with a combined total Challenge effect cost of no Might.  (The development is destroyed, you gain +2 Coin for Sacking, and you take control of the Region.)
- **As Blood** — Bloodriders may only include Drogo, Rakharo, Qotho, Cohollo, or Haggo.  Any of these characters who were removed from the tray and not restored by the end of the game must make an Injury Save.  If this unit is deployed without a full complement of these characters, then it suffers pre-existing Wounds.

## Grey Worm
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Unsullied Focus** — If Grey Worm participates in a battle, Targaryen Commanders and Attachments gain +1 to Death and Capture Saves.  If Dragon's Dominance is in effect, these Characters are immune to negative modifiers for Death and Capture Saves instead.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Haggo
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Whip Focus** — If Haggo participates in the battle, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.  If Field Warfare is in effect, they suffer -2 to their Capture Save instead.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Hizdahr Zo Loraq
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** NCU
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Foreign Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle was a victory, Gain +1 Coin.

## Illyrio Mopatis
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** NCU
- **Safe Refuge** — When Illyrio participates a battle and is still in play at the conclusion, one friendly Character may re-roll one failed Capture save at the conclusion of that battle.
- **Foreign Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle was a victory, Gain +1 Coin.

## Jorah Mormont
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment/Solo
- **Knight's Focus** — If Jorah participates in a battle and is still in play at the end of the game, choose one friendly character who was off the board at the end of the game, that character makes an Injury Save instead of being automatically injured.  Jorah is always entitled to an Injury Save after using Whatever May Come.  If Field Warfare is in effect, Saves from this Trait may be re-rolled.
- **Exile's Journey** — When Jorah participates in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), he is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.  Once per Campaign Jorah's Commander version may ignore location restrictions during Challenge Phase.

## Khal Drogo
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Commander
- **Deadly Foe** — If this character's army wins and this character is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.

## Marselen
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Rigorous Discipline** — When this character participates in a battle, friendly Characters are immune to the effects of (2) Drunken Revelry.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll a Capture Save.
- **Naathi Marshall** — When Marselen Commanding in a Sea Battle, Unsullied units suffer -1 Wound from the Torrent keyword.  When Marselen participates in a battle, Securing costs no Might.

## Mirri Mazduur
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Life Must Pay for Life** — When Mirri participates in a battle, after rolling Injury, Death, and Capture saves for that battle, choose one other friendly and one enemy Character.  Both Mirri and that Character must make Death saves and the enemy Character suffers -1 to their roll.
- **Easily Overlooked** — This character is immune to any negative effects against their Capture Saves.

## Missandei
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** NCU
- **Queen's Mouthpiece** — Once per Campaign during Council Phase, if Missandei participated in a battle the prior Month which was a victory and Targaryens now own the Capital in the that Realm, double the effect of Revealed Loyalty.
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Pyat Pree
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** NCU
- **Irresistible Trap** — Take note of any enemy Character who Claims the House of the Undying zone. At the conclusion of that game, they must make a Capture Save.  If they Claimed it more than once, they suffer -1 to that Capture Save for each additional time they Claimed it.
- **Element of Surprise** — If this Character's army wins and this Character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.

## Qotho

- **Type:** Attachment
- **Bow Focus** — If Qotho participates in the battle, choose 1 enemy character who in play at the end of the game, they suffer an Injury Save with -1 to their roll. If Field Warfare is in effect, they suffer an Injury Save with -2 to their roll instead.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Quaithe
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** NCU
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Feminine Wiles** — If this is still on the board at the end of the game, choose 1 uninjured enemy character, they must make a Capture Save with -1 to their roll.

## Rakharo
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Interceptor** — When this character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Skahaz Mo Kandaq
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Harpy's Focus** — If Skahaz's army wins and he is still in play at the end of the battle, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.  If City Pacification is in effect and Shakaz is in Command, that Character suffers -2 to their Capture Save instead.
- **Warrior Noble** — When this Character participates in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle was a victory, Gain +2 Coin.

## Xaro Xhoan Daxos
- **Faction:** Targaryen
- **Type:** NCU
- **Aristocratic Focus** — If City Pacification is in effect and Xaro Xhoan Daxos participates in a battle, gain +1 Coin for each participating Targaryen NCU who was not Injured, captured, or killed at the end of the battle.
- **Foreign Noble** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Might.  If the battle was a victory, Gain +1 Coin.
