# Nights Watch Character Traits V1.1

## Faction Trait: Night'S Watch Faction Trait
Enduring Oathkeeper - This Character gains +1 to Death Saves Beyond the Wall (Realm), +1 to Injury Saves at the Wall (Realm), or +1 to Capture Saves in all other Realms.  If this Character participates in a battle as a Commander, gain +1 Might if it took place Beyond the Wall (Realm) or at the Wall (Realm), and +1 Coin if it took place in any other Realm.

## Aemon
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** NCU
- **Value of the Oath** — Each time Aemon participates in a battle: if this was not a team battle, gain Aemon +3.  If this was a team battle and your Ally faction has previously contributed Might to the Wall, gain Aemon +1. If Aemon was not Captured at the conclusion of this battle you and you and your Ally each gain +3 Might on victory.
- **Experienced Healer** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  The character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.

## Alliser Thorne
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Deadly Foe** — If this character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who not in play at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.
- **Rigorous Discipline** — When this character participates in a battle, friendly Characters are immune to the effects of (2) Drunken Revelry.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll a Capture Save.

## Benjen Stark
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Lost Ranger** — Once per Campaign when Commanding, Benjen gains the following which must be applied to separate units in this army: Ranger Unit +1, Ranger Unit +1, Ranger Unit +1.  At the conclusion of this battle Benjen does not roll a Death Save and instead immediately becomes Coldhands.
- **Intrepid Guide** — When this character is Commanding they may pass through 1 region of impassible terrain during Challenge Phase.

## Bowen Marsh
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** NCU
- **Donated Stockpiles** — Once per campaign during Council Phase if Bowen participated in a Team Battle during the previous Month, your ally faction from that battle may contribute +1 additional Might to restore one additional abandoned castle this Month.
- **Capable Stewardship** — When this character participates as an NCU and is still in play after the battle, gain +1 Coin.

## Coldhands
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Mysterious Savior** — Coldhands may not be fielded until Benjen Stark has been removed from the Campaign.  When Coldhands participates in a battle Beyond the Wall and is on the board at the resolution of the battle, select one other friendly character who is performing an Injury or Capture Save.  That character may re-roll that save.
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Cotter Pyke
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **Sworn Sailor** — When Cotter begins Challenge Phase in a Sea Region, he may move through two Sea Regions instead of one when Challenging.  When Cotter is Commanding in Sea Battles, gain Cotter Pyke +3.
- **Privateer Tactics** — When this Character participates in a battle and was still in play at the conclusion of the battle: gain +2 Might if this was an Intercept Challenge for your faction, and an additional +2 Might if you were victorious. Gain +2 Coin for Sacking.

## Craster
- **Faction:** Night's Watch / Free Folk
- **Type:** NCU
- **Vital Shelter** — If Craster is controlled by Night's Watch and not Captured or Dead during Council Phase, for each Nights Watch Commander in the Beyond the Wall (Realm), gain +1 Might.
- **Craster's Keep** — Craster may only be fielded by one faction at a time, Free Folk or Night's Watch, when they control the Haunted Forest.

## Donal Noye
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Scarecrow Sentinels** — When Donal is Commanding a defensive battle your opponent(s) total Might Rank bonus against you is halved (round down).  Once per Campaign when Commanding, Donal gains the following which must be applied to separate Night's Watch units in this army: Named Attachment +1, Named Attachment +1, Named Attachment +1.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Eddison Tollett
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Probably Me** — When Eddison is not in play at the end of a battle he is not automatically Injured and is entitled to an Injury Save before Death/Capture Saves as normal.
- **Sad Steward** — If Eddison participates in a battle, was victorious, and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +1 Coin.

## Gilly
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** NCU
- **Advocates of the Watch** — When Sam and Gilly are deployed as NCUs together they are not bound by location restrictions and do not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Feminine Wiles** — If this character is still on the board at the end of the battle, choose enemy Character still in play, they must make a Capture Save with -1 to their roll.

## Grenn
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Steadfast Friends** — When this character participates in a battle with another Character who has this trait and is still in play at the end of the battle, gain +1 Might.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Jeor Mormont
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Lord at the Wall** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, gain +2 Might.  If the battle took place in the Wall (Realm), gain an additional +2 Might.
- **Sworn Marshall** — When Jeor is Commanding in a defensive battle, gain Jeor Mormont +3 and Securing costs no Might.

## Jon Snow
- **Faction:** Night's Watch / Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/Attachment
- **True Crow** — The first time Jon participates in any Realm outside the Wall, at the conclusion of the battle he does not roll any Saves and becomes a Free Folk Character.  If the Free Folk Trait Crow Come Over causes him to become a Night's Watch Character again, when Jon is Commanding gain Jon Snow +3.
- **Brought Back** — The first time Jon fails a Death Save, he is not Dead or Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.  After triggering this effect, immediately rebuild 2 abandoned castles at the Wall for no Might Cost

## Othell Yarwyck
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Commander/ Attachment/NCU
- **Builders of the Watch** — After the Night's Watch player rolls an Event, if a (13) or (17) is rolled you may ignore that stated effect.  Instead, you may use this Challenge to move Othell onto one of the Wall's Regions that you own if he is eligible to do so.  You may then expend 1 Might to upgrade an abandoned castle in that Region, ignoring the normal restrictions if you have already upgraded one this Month.  You may then immediately use another Commander to Challenge another Region as normal.
- **Siegemaster** — Once per Campaign when Commanding, Othell gains the following which must be applied to separate units in this army: Builder Unit +1, Builder Unit +1, Builder Unit +1.  Each time Othell is Commanding as a defender in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Rapid Repairs card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.

## Pypar
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Steadfast Friends** — When this character participates in a battle with another Character who has this trait and is still in play at the end of the battle, gain +1 Might.
- **Easily Overlooked** — This Character is immune to any negative effects against their Capture Saves.

## Qhorin Halfhand
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Wildling's Bane** — After the Night's Watch player rolls an Event, if Qhorin is available you may replace that Event with (8) Lying in Wait, then select any Commander and proceed with your Challenge.  Note that Qhorin must be included in this battle.
- **Heavy Cost** — If Qhorin was out of play at the end of the battle due to Sacrifice for the Cause he suffers -2 to his Death Save but does not suffer a Capture Save on success. Gain +2 Might if he passes this Save and +4 Might if he fails.

## Samwell Tarly
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Rudimentary Healing** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a battle and is in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  That character gains +1 to one of those tests.
- **Advocates of the Watch** — When Sam and Gilly are deployed as NCUs together they are not bound by location restrictions and do not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Satin
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Rudimentary Healing** — When this character participates as an Attachment in a battle and is in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  The character gains +1 to one of those tests.
- **Easily Overlooked** — This Character is immune to any negative effects against their Capture Saves.

## Ser Denys Mallister
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Commander/NCU
- **Machines from the Shadow Tower** — Each time Denys is Commanding as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Catapult Barrage card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck.
- **Chivalrous Virtue** — When this character is Commanding, Sack may not be chosen by this faction and Conquering costs no Might.  Once per Campaign this Character may re-roll one die while fighting in a Trial by Combat.

## Yoren
- **Faction:** Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment/NCU
- **Local Conscription** — Each time Yoren participates in a battle as an Attachment in a Realm other than the Wall or Beyond the Wall: Gain Yoren +1 and gain Conscripts +1 if that Region's controlling faction has contributed Might to the Night's Watch.
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
