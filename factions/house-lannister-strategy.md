# House Lannister - Strategy Sheet

## Faction Overview
As the richest house in Westeros, the Lannisters use wealth to dominate their rivals. To represent the gold mines beneath Casterly Rock, during Council Phase privately roll a d6 and gain that amount of Coin. On a 6, add +6 Coin but then discontinue this effect for the rest of the Campaign, allowing opponents of the Lannisters to believe that the mines beneath Casterly Rock have not run dry. Unlike other factions, the Lannisters do not reveal their total Coin unless requested to do so by a Referee.

## Unique Mechanics

### Flaunted Wealth
During Council Phase the Lannisters may voluntarily declare their current total Coin. If no other faction has a greater amount of Coin than the Lannisters at that time, immediately gain Might equal to the difference between the Lannisters' Coin and the next richest faction up to +10 Might.

### Mercenary Monopoly
The Lannisters must conduct an auction of all available Neutral Characters during Council Phase each month. This auction may be performed in any manner as long as all other factions have an opportunity to bid their maximum desired Coin on any available Neutral character using Might Rank to break ties. The Lannisters may choose to outbid their opponents for any character using their own Coin before moving onto the next character for auction. The Lannisters gain +2 to their army list size for each level of Coin Bonus they purchase (+2, +4, +6 instead of +1, +2, +3).

## Power Play (Monthly Objectives)
At the end of each listed Month you may declare the following effects if the associated Characters are available to you.

- **End of Month 1:** Dismiss Barristan Selmy from the Kingsguard, causing him to join the Targaryens. **Gain +5 Might.**
- **End of Month 2:** Send Arys Oakheart on a royal escort to Dorne, causing him to join the Martells. **Gain +5 Might.**
- **End of Month 3:** Cause Sandor Clegane to desert his post, making him a Neutral character and hireable during Council Phase. **Gain +5 Might.**
- **End of Month 4:** If you hold King's Landing, blow up the Great Sept of Baelor using wildfire, killing the High Sparrow and removing your access to Warrior's Sons, Poor Fellows and Champions of the Faith. **Gain +10 Might.**
- **End of Month 5:** If you hold King's Landing, levy an inordinate tax on the populace of the city. **Gain +10 Coin.**
- **End of Month 6:** Bask in the supremacy of the Lannister Dynasty. Count every faction that has fewer Total Coin than you at the end of Month 6. **Gain +2 Might to House Lannister for each of those factions.**

## Special Rules: The Power of Coin
These effects cost -2 Coin and may each be triggered once during Council Phase or once per Campaign.

- **House Stark (Suspicious Reconciliation):** Double the bonuses of Homefront Banners until the end of this Month.
- **House Greyjoy (Bribe Shipwrights):** Unlock the next Iron Fleet slot (in order).
- **House Lannister (Inflated Promises):** After gaining Might from Flaunted Wealth, immediately gain that much Might again.
- **Brotherhood Without Banners (Bandit Racketeering):** Gain double Justice from each Character surviving your next battle and double Justice for winning that battle.
- **House Martell (Opportunistic Extortion):** Change the target faction of one Intrigue while keeping its progress.
- **House Baratheon (Blackmail Nobles):** Double the Legitimacy benefit of Sworn Houses this Month.
- **House Targaryen (Tribute to the Dragon):** Commanders in Reserve generate +1 Might from Depth of Command next Month.
- **House Bolton (Ruthless Co-conspirator):** One Commander gains +2 Schemes.
- **Night's Watch (Donation to the Wall):** Unlock one additional Abandoned Castle this Month for no Might cost.
- **Free Folk (Baubles for Wildlings):** One Motivation Track counts as having one additional Commander this Month.
