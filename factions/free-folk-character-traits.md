# Free Folk Character Traits V1.1

## Faction Trait: Free Folk Faction Trait
The Coming Doom - This Character ignores impassible terrain on the Campaign Map.  For every two Characters with this Trait still in play at the end of a battle, choose one enemy Character from that battle.  They suffer -1 to Death Saves.  Each enemy Character may only be targeted by one instance of The Coming Doom per battle.

## The Bonelord's Chosen
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Unit
- **Woe to Kneelers** — If this unit takes part in a battle Beyond the Wall, gain +1 Might.  If this unit takes part in  a battle anywhere else, gain +2 Might.  If this unit survives the battle, gain +1 Might for each Rattleshirt Tactics Card attached to this unit.
- **Handpicked Men** — Record how many Wounds this unit has sustained at the end of the battle.  If this unit participates in a battle next Month, it begins that battle with the name number of suffered Wounds.

## Borroq
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Man and Beast** — If Borroq and Borroq's Boar participate in a battle and both are in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.
- **Interceptor** — When this Character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.

## Craster
- **Faction:** Free Folk / Night's Watch
- **Type:** NCU
- **Craster's Keep** — Craster may only be fielded by one faction at a time, Free Folk or Night's Watch, when they control the Haunted Forest.
- **Appeasing Death** — Craster is immune to negative modifiers to Injury and Death Saves.

## The Great Walrus
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ Attachment
- **Whalebone Barges** — When the Great Walrus is Commanding in a Sea Battle, Frozen Shore units suffer -1 Wound from the Torrent keyword and Free Folk Characters are immune to negative modifiers on Capture Saves.
- **Shoreline Marshall** — When the Great Walrus is Commanding, Securing costs no Might.  When he is Commanding in an island or coastal Region, enemies may not gain Might Rank bonuses against him.

## Harma
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this unit is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Plunderer** — When this character is Commanding, Sacking costs no Might and gains +2 additional Coin.

## Jarl
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Attachment
- **Traveler** — When participating in a battle as an Attachment (non-Commander), this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Interceptor** — When this Character participates in a battle where their army is intercepting the enemy Commander and this Character is still in play in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might.  On a victory, gain +1 Coin.

## Jon Snow
- **Faction:** Free Folk / Night's Watch
- **Type:** Attachment/ NCU
- **Crow Come Over** — When Jon is made a Free Folk Character by True Crow he remains a Free Folk Character until two Council Phases have concluded, or until the first time he participates in a battle as a Free Folk Character and is out of play at the conclusion of the battle, whichever occurs first.  He does not roll Injury, Death, or Capture Saves.
- **Test of Loyalty** — If Jon is deployed by the Free Folk in the Wall Realm, the Free Folk gain +5 Might and an additional +5 Might on victory.

## Lady Dalla
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** NCU
- **Experienced Healer** — When this character participates as an NCU in a battle and is still in play at its conclusion, prior to rolling Injury or Death saves but after your enemy declares any negative modifiers to those tests, declare one friendly character.  That character gains +1 to those tests for the resolution of this battle.
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Lady Val
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** NCU
- **Advocate Abroad** — When participating in a battle as an NCU, this Character is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.
- **Feminine Wiles** — If this character is still on the board at the end of the battle, choose one enemy Character still in play, they must make a Capture Save with -1 to their roll.

## Mag the Mighty
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ Solo
- **Last Chieftain of Giants** — When Mag the Mighty is placed in the Old Ways Motivation he may occupy two positions in that track.  Mag may not participate in (20) Lavish Tourney and may not be a Champion in a Trial by Combat unless he is the also the Captive.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Mance Rayder
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ Attachment/  NCU
- **The Growing Host** — When Mance is Commanding and is in play at the conclusion of the battle: Conquer costs no Might.  When Mance is Commanding and the Free Folk are defenders in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Defender deck for one Unexpected Reinforcements card and draw it.  Shuffle the Defender deck.
- **Escaping the Long Night** — When Mance is placed in the Desperate Migration Motivation he may occupy two positions in that track.  When Mance Rayder, Abel the Bard Participates in a battle and is out of play at its conclusion, make a Capture Save instead of a Death Save.  Once per Campaign you may re-roll this Capture Save.

## Rattleshirt
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ Attachment
- **Counting Skulls** — When Rattleshirt is in play at the conclusion of the battle, gain +1 Might for each enemy Combat unit destroyed by an Attack from Rattleshirt's unit during the battle.
- **Bones of the Kneelers** — When Rattleshirt is placed on the Death to Kneelers Motivation he may occupy two positions in that track.

## Styr
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ NCU
- **Deadly Foe** — If this character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 enemy character who was off the board at the end of the game, they suffer -1 to their Death Save.
- **Ruinous Rage** — When Styr is Commanding as an attacker in a Break the Siege battle, after deployment search the Attacker deck for one Sack the Castle card and draw it.  Shuffle the Attacker deck

## Tormund Giantsbane
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ Attachment
- **Braggadocious Titles** — Once per Campaign, after Month 1, when Tormund is placed on any Motivation track in the Horde United, he may occupy two positions on that track.
- **Frontline Warrior** — The first time this character passes a Death Save, this character is not Injured and does not roll a Capture Save.

## Varamyr Sixskins
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Commander/ Solo
- **Element of Surprise** — If this character's army wins and this character is still in play at the end of the game, choose 1 Injured enemy character, they suffer -1 to their Capture Save.
- **Wayfaring Warg** — When Varamyr participates in a battle as a Solo (non-Commander), this unit is not bound by location restrictions and does not count as moving to the Region of this battle during this Month.

## Wun Wun
- **Faction:** Free Folk
- **Type:** Solo
- **Chieftain's Son** — If Mag the Might is Dead, Injured, or Captured but Wun Wun is not, Wun Wun may be placed on The Old Ways Motivation during Council Phase, occupying one position.  Wun Wun may not participate in (20) Lavish Tourney and may not be a Champion in a Trial by Combat unless he is the also the Captive.
